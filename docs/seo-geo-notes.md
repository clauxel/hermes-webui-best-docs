# SEO and GEO Notes

## Entity Definition

Hermes WebUI Launch Lab is a Hermes WebUI deployment readiness scanner and report workflow at https://hermes-webui.best/.

## First-Answer Summary

Hermes WebUI Launch Lab scans redacted deployment plans and turns install, Docker, WSL2, SSH, gateway, and provider risks into launch receipts.

## Human Utility

The public pages explain features, workflow, use cases, guides, docs, GitHub context, pricing, support, and limits. They avoid copied template content and name the real product category.

## Important Boundaries

- Readiness reports are planning aids and do not certify security or guarantee runtime success.
- Never paste live provider keys, cookies, or full private environment files into the scanner.
- Third-party model providers, browsers, tunnels, and hosts can change behavior outside the product boundary.
- Hermes WebUI Launch Lab is independent and does not claim official Hermes Agent, Nous Research, or upstream project endorsement.
